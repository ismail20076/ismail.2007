Vehical system

A Vehicle System in Roblox Studio uses a VehicleSeat to capture player inputs (WASD) and translate them into physical movement. It relies on Constraints, such as HingeConstraints for motors and SpringConstraints for suspension, to simulate realistic driving physics. Most developers use a Chassis (the framework) to coordinate these parts and handle features like steering, speed, and braking.
